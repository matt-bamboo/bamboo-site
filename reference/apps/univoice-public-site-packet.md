## Public Positioning

**App name:** UniVoice

**Tagline:** Sing. Score. Grow.

**Short description:**  
UniVoice is a kids’ karaoke and singing coach app with real-time pitch feedback, song practice, warmups, and playful rewards.

**Long description:**  
UniVoice turns singing practice into a game for kids. Children can choose from familiar public-domain songs, follow scrolling lyrics, see real-time pitch feedback, earn stars, and build confidence with warmups and ear-training games. Parents can manage profiles, review progress, and keep singing practice fun and age-appropriate.

## Target Users

UniVoice is built for:

- Kids ages 4–12 who like singing or karaoke
- Parents looking for skill-building screen time
- Families who want kid-friendly songs and feedback
- Young singers practicing pitch matching, warmups, and confidence
- Music teachers or caregivers looking for a simple vocal practice tool

## Core Value Proposition

Most karaoke apps are designed for adults or offer entertainment without useful feedback. UniVoice is designed for children, with forgiving pitch feedback, playful coaching, warmups, and kid-friendly songs.

## Public Feature Summary

### Kid-friendly song library

UniVoice includes a built-in catalog of public-domain songs, organized by category.

### Real-time pitch feedback

A visual pitch bar helps children see how close they are to each note while they sing.

### Scrolling lyrics

Lyrics move with the song to help kids follow along.

### Star scoring and progress

Children earn stars, achievements, and progress markers as they practice.

### Coach personas

Kids can choose friendly coach characters that encourage practice and improvement.

### Warmups and ear training

Guided vocal warmups and pitch-recognition games support skill-building before singing.

### Vocal range calibration

The app can adapt songs to a child’s comfortable singing range.

### Parent Zone

Parents can manage profiles, review progress, adjust settings, and manage saved recordings.

### FAQ

**Why does UniVoice need microphone access?**  
UniVoice listens while a child sings so it can show pitch feedback and score the performance. Pitch analysis runs on the device in the current implementation.

**Does UniVoice record my child’s voice?**  
Voice recordings should only be saved when the user chooses to save them. Confirm exact shipped behavior before publishing final language.

**Does progress sync across devices?**  
No. Current progress is stored locally on the device. Deleting the app or switching devices may remove progress and recordings.

**How do I access the Parent Zone?**  
Use the Parent Zone entry point and complete the parent gate. Parent controls are intended to protect settings and recordings from accidental changes.

**Why are some songs locked?**  
Some songs may be part of a premium or planned subscription experience. Do not publish pricing or subscription details until live billing is confirmed.

**What happens if the AI coach voice does not work?**  
Some coach voice features may require internet access. The app may fall back to device speech when the server or voice service is unavailable.

**How do I delete a profile or recording?**  
Profiles and recordings can be managed from profile settings or the Parent Zone in the current implementation. Confirm exact paths before publishing final instructions.

## Privacy Page Inputs

Use cautious privacy language. UniVoice is child-directed and requires legal/privacy review before public launch.

### Privacy positioning

UniVoice is designed as a child-friendly singing app with local profiles and on-device pitch feedback. Some optional coach voice features may use third-party voice generation.

### Data users may enter or create

UniVoice may store or process:

- Child first name or profile name
- Child age or age range
- Avatar, theme, or coach preferences
- Song performance history
- Stars, scores, and timestamps
- Vocal range calibration values
- Favorite songs
- Optional saved voice recordings
- Volume and app settings
- Subscription or trial state if billing is enabled

### Local storage

Profiles, progress, preferences, favorites, and recordings are stored locally on the device in the current implementation.

### On-device processing

Pitch detection and scoring run locally on the device in the current implementation.

### Third-party processing

Some coach voice features may send short coach-summary text to a third-party voice generation provider to create audio. Do not include final provider or retention claims without confirming the live implementation and legal review.

### Accounts

UniVoice currently does not require account creation or sign-in.

### Children

UniVoice is intended for children and requires careful privacy review before public launch. Do not claim COPPA compliance without legal review.

### Analytics and tracking
