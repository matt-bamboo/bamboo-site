## Public Positioning

**App name:** TripTracker Pro

**Tagline:** AI-powered resort receipt tracking

**Short description:**  
TripTracker Pro helps resort travelers scan receipts, track expenses, reconcile hotel bills, split costs, and keep a clean record of their trip.

**Long description:**  
TripTracker Pro turns the financial chaos of a resort vacation into a clear, organized record. Travelers can photograph receipts, review AI-extracted line items, compare saved receipts against a hotel folio, track shared expenses, and keep notes about resorts and staff. The app is designed for travelers who want to catch billing mistakes, understand where money went, and leave with a reliable trip summary.

## Target Users

TripTracker Pro is built for:

- Frequent resort and timeshare travelers
- Travelers managing multi-person trips
- People who want to reconcile hotel folios before checkout
- Travelers who track cash tips, card charges, and shared expenses
- Resort guests who want notes and records to carry forward to future stays

## Core Value Proposition

Resort trips create scattered financial records: paper receipts, room-charge slips, cash tips, hotel folio line items, and shared expenses. TripTracker Pro gives travelers one place to organize those records and check whether the final bill makes sense.

## Public Feature Summary

### Receipt capture and review

Users can photograph receipts and review extracted details such as venue, date, line items, tip, and total. Manual correction is available when a scan is imperfect.

### Hotel folio reconciliation

Users can upload hotel bill or folio images and compare line items against saved receipts. This helps identify charges that are matched, accepted, or need review.

### Expense and cash tracking

Users can log card expenses, cash expenses, receipt-linked tips, and trip costs.

### Shared trip ledger

For group trips, users can track who paid what and review a settlement summary.

### Staff and tip history

Users can keep a people directory for resort staff and track tip history across trips.

### Resort notes

Users can save notes about a resort so useful details carry forward to future visits.

### Flight and trip details

Users can track flight information, confirmation details, trip dates, currency settings, and budget information.

### Export and import

Users can export trip records as JSON files and import them later. This is the current path for moving data between devices.

## Support Page Draft

### FAQ

**How do I scan a receipt?**  
Open the receipt scanning flow, take a clear photo, review the extracted fields, correct anything that looks wrong, and save.

**Can I fix an AI extraction mistake?**  
Yes. Open the receipt and edit the incorrect fields manually.

**How does folio reconciliation work?**  
Upload a hotel folio or final bill. TripTracker Pro compares the folio line items against receipts and expenses you saved during the trip.

**Does TripTracker Pro sync across devices?**  
No. Current trip data is stored locally on the device. Export/import is the current way to move data.

**How do I move data to a new phone?**  
Export your trip data as a JSON file, transfer it to the new phone, and import it in the app.

**Does the app work offline?**  
Many tracking features can work without an internet connection. AI-powered scanning, folio extraction, assistant features, and weather-related features require internet access.

**What happens if I delete a trip?**  
Deleting a trip is intended to permanently remove that trip’s records from the app. Confirm current shipped behavior before publishing exact deletion instructions.

**What is Demo Mode?**  
Demo Mode uses sample trip data so users can explore features without affecting their own trips.

### Known limitations to disclose

- No cross-device sync currently
- No account recovery because there is no account system
- AI extraction depends on image quality and receipt format
- Exchange rates are user-configured and may not match actual transaction rates
- Manual export/import is required to move records between devices

## Privacy Page Inputs

Use cautious privacy language. Do not claim that no data ever leaves the device, because AI-powered features transmit content for processing.

### Privacy positioning

TripTracker Pro is designed as a personal, device-centered trip tracker. Most trip records are stored locally on the user’s device. Some optional AI-powered features require sending receipt, folio, flight, or assistant content to an external AI service for processing.

### Data users may enter or create

TripTracker Pro may store:

- Trip names, dates, resort details, room details, and traveler names
- Receipt amounts, item names, tips, totals, and currencies
- Receipt photos and hotel folio photos
- Cash expenses and shared ledger entries
- Staff names, staff roles, and tip history
- Flight details and confirmation information
- Resort notes and saved places
- App preferences
- AI assistant messages and uploaded context

### Local storage

Trip records, receipts, expenses, ledger entries, notes, people records, and preferences are stored on the user’s device. Receipt and folio images may be stored in the device file system.

### Third-party processing

AI-powered features may transmit relevant content to a third-party AI provider for processing. This can include receipt images, folio images, flight confirmation images, extracted text, or assistant messages.

Weather-related features may use a weather or geocoding service based on resort location information.

### Accounts

TripTracker Pro currently does not require a user account or sign-in.

### Analytics and tracking

### Children
