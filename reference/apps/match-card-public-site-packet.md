## Public Positioning

**App name:** Match Card

**Tagline:** Golf scorecard and round analytics

**Short description:**  
Match Card is a two-player golf scorecard for iPhone that helps regular playing partners score rounds, track rivalry stats, settle friendly side games, and keep a course-by-course record of how they play.

**Long description:**  
Match Card is built for the golfers who play the same courses with the same regular partner and care about the rivalry as much as the score. It keeps live scoring simple, supports tap or voice score entry, tracks per-course performance, calculates a course-specific handicap, and keeps a season-long head-to-head trophy. The app is local-first, works offline for scoring, and does not require an account.

## Target Users

Match Card is built for:

- Recreational golfers who play regularly with the same partner
- Two-player golf rivalries
- Golfers who care about course-specific history and head-to-head records
- Players who want a cleaner alternative to bloated golf apps
- Golfers who want side-game math handled for them

## Core Value Proposition

Most golf apps are built around broad social features, generic scorecards, or full tournament systems. Match Card focuses on the round you actually play: two regular players, familiar courses, fast scoring, friendly wagers, and a long-running season trophy.

## Public Feature Summary

### Live round scoring

Score hole by hole with quick tap controls. Drafts auto-save during a round so scoring can continue if the phone is pocketed or the app is reopened.

### Voice score entry

Users can tap the microphone and speak the scores for the current hole. Match Card transcribes and parses the score entry. This is tap-to-record, not always-listening.

### Per-course handicap

Match Card tracks performance separately by course, giving golfers a course-specific view instead of blending every round into one generic number.

### Season trophy

A customizable season-long head-to-head trophy tracks results between the two primary players.

### Side games

Match Card supports friendly side games such as Skins, Match Play, Nassau, Stableford, Wolf, Bingo Bango Bongo, Closest to the Pin, Longest Drive, Greenies, Sandies, First Birdie, First Par, and Press.

### Bag and club notes

Users can maintain a club inventory with typical distances and notes, helping inform course and tee decisions over time.

### Course and hole insights

Match Card builds course memory from the user’s own rounds, including hole-by-hole history, recent trends, and notes.

### Stats, records, and share cards

Users can review round history, scoring distribution, personal records, course analysis, and generate a share card after a round.

### Optional backup

Backup is optional and off by default. Users can export or restore app data using a device key. There is no account system.

### FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. Match Card does not require sign-in or account creation.

**Which devices are supported?**  
Match Card is currently built for iPhone. Do not claim iPad, Android, web, or Apple Watch support unless confirmed.

**How does voice scoring work?**  
Tap the microphone on the live scoring screen and say the scores. It is a tap-to-record feature, not always-listening.

**Why does the app ask for location?**  
Location may be used to capture a weather stamp for the round or bias course search. If location is denied, the app should still support scoring.

**Why does the app ask for microphone access?**  
The microphone is used only when the user taps to record a voice score entry.

**Why does the app ask for photo or camera access?**  
Photos or camera may be used for optional round photos or scorecard scanning features.

**Is my data backed up?**  
Only if the user enables optional backup. Backup is off by default.

**How do I move data to a new phone?**  
Use the optional backup/restore flow with the device key if enabled. Without backup, data is local to the original device.

**How do I delete everything?**  
Use the app’s erase/reset flow for local data. Optional cloud backup, if enabled, should be deleted separately through the backup screen.

**Does Match Card handle money or betting payments?**  
No. Match Card can tally friendly side games, but it does not process payments, transfer money, or settle wagers.

## Privacy Page Inputs

Use precise local-first privacy language. Do not claim that data never leaves the device, because optional features can send audio, scorecard images, location, or course-search information to service providers.

### Privacy positioning

Match Card is local-first and account-free. Most scoring, stats, handicap, course, and settings data lives on the user’s device. Some optional features use third-party services only to perform the feature the user requested.

### Data stored on device

Match Card may store:

- Round scores and scoring details
- Player names
- Course and venue records
- Hole notes and course history
- Bag and club data
- Side-game results
- App settings
- Attached local photo references
- Derived stats, records, and handicap calculations

### Optional backup

If the user enables backup, Match Card may store an opaque backup copy of app data on a Bamboo-operated server, keyed by a device key rather than a user account. Backup is optional and should be described as off by default unless owner confirms otherwise.

### Data processed by third parties

Depending on which features the user chooses, Match Card may send limited data to service providers:

- Voice clips for transcription and score parsing
- Scorecard images for parsing
- Course search queries and approximate location for course lookup
- Approximate coordinates for weather information

These features should be described as feature-specific processing, not advertising or tracking.

### Accounts

Match Card currently does not require user accounts, login, email, Apple Sign In, or Google Sign In.

### Analytics and tracking

### Payments

### Children

### Deletion
