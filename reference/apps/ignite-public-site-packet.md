## Public Positioning

**App name:** Ignite

**Tagline:** Know your number. Own your timeline.

**Short description:**  
Ignite helps people pursuing financial independence track their runway, net worth, spending, and FIRE progress with account-aware planning tools and an AI coach.

**Long description:**  
Ignite is a personal finance app built for people serious about financial independence. It helps users connect accounts, track net worth, review transactions, model FIRE scenarios, and understand what their current spending means for their retirement timeline. Ignite adapts to the user’s phase, whether they are building toward financial independence or already living off a portfolio, and provides an AI coach for plain-English financial explanations.

Ignite is a planning and education tool. It is not a licensed financial advisor, investment advisor, tax advisor, legal advisor, or insurance broker.

## Target Users

Ignite is built for:

- Adults pursuing financial independence or early retirement
- FIRE-focused savers and investors
- People who want more than a monthly budget app
- Retirees or near-retirees who want drawdown visibility
- Users who want to understand runway, safe withdrawal rates, net worth, and spending drift

## Core Value Proposition

Most personal finance apps show what users spent. Ignite connects daily financial data to long-term independence planning. It helps users answer: “How long will my money last, and what does today’s spending do to my timeline?”

## Public Feature Summary

### Runway tracking

Ignite shows how long a user’s current savings may last based on spending, portfolio size, and planning assumptions.

### FIRE number and progress

Users can estimate a FIRE target and track progress toward financial independence.

### Account and transaction visibility

Ignite can connect bank and brokerage accounts through Plaid, giving users a clearer view of balances, transactions, categories, and trends.

### Net worth tracking

Users can track assets, accounts, liabilities, and net worth over time.

### Persona-driven experience

Ignite adapts vocabulary, dashboard depth, and tool visibility based on the user’s planning style and phase.

### AI financial coach

Ignite includes an AI coach that can answer questions using the user’s financial context. Responses are informational only and should not be treated as professional advice.

### Advanced planning tools

Ignite may include FIRE calculators, safe withdrawal rate tracking, Monte Carlo projections, Roth conversion modeling, tax-loss harvesting estimates, goals, bills, and Medicare-readiness planning.

### Demo mode

Users can explore the app with fictional data before connecting real accounts.

### Data export and deletion

Users should be able to export their data and delete their account/data from within the app, subject to final shipped UI confirmation.

### FAQ

**Do I need to connect a bank account?**  
No. Demo Mode lets users explore Ignite with fictional financial data before connecting real accounts.

**How does account connection work?**  
Ignite uses Plaid to connect supported financial institutions. Users authenticate through Plaid's flow. Ignite does not receive the user's bank sign-in details.

**What is runway?**  
Runway is an estimate of how long money may last based on current balances, spending, and assumptions.

**What is a FIRE number?**  
A FIRE number is an estimated portfolio target associated with financial independence. It is commonly based on annual expenses and a safe withdrawal assumption.

**Does Ignite give financial advice?**  
No. Ignite provides calculators, projections, and AI-assisted educational information. It does not provide licensed financial, tax, investment, insurance, or legal advice.

**Does the AI coach use my financial data?**  
Yes. When users interact with the AI coach, Ignite may send financial summaries, spending context, account information, or planning metrics to an AI service to generate a response.

**Can I delete my data?**  
The app is designed to allow account and data deletion from Settings. Final support copy should match the shipped UI.

**Can I export my data?**  
The app is designed to support data export. Final support copy should match the shipped UI.

**Why is my bank connection not updating?**  
Some Plaid connections need periodic reauthorization. Users may need to reconnect the institution or refresh account data.

**Is there a web version?**  
Do not claim a public web version unless owner confirms. Treat Ignite as a native mobile app.

## Privacy Page Inputs

Use cautious privacy language. Ignite handles sensitive financial data and uses third-party services.

### Data users may provide or connect

Ignite may process:

- Name and email address from sign-in provider
- Birthdate or age-related planning inputs
- State or region for certain planning estimates
- Annual income, annual expenses, savings targets, FIRE assumptions, and planning preferences
- Bank account balances, account types, transaction history, merchants, dates, categories, and amounts via Plaid
- Manual assets such as real estate, vehicles, collectibles, or crypto holdings
- Bills, goals, investment trades, and other user-entered records
- AI chat messages and voice interaction transcripts
- Subscription status and purchase-related records through app-store billing infrastructure
- App preferences and dashboard settings

### Storage

Ignite is a server-backed app. User data may be stored in a backend database so users can access their account across devices. Sensitive financial connection details should be described cautiously and only after confirming the shipped system.

### Third-party services

Ignite may use:

- Clerk for authentication
- Plaid for bank account connectivity
- Google Gemini or another AI provider for AI coaching
- RevenueCat, Apple, and Google for subscription and purchase management
- CoinGecko for crypto price lookup
- Partner/referral systems if partner offers are enabled

### AI processing

When users ask the AI coach a question or use AI-powered features, Ignite may send relevant financial context and user messages to an AI provider to generate responses. This should be disclosed clearly. Do not claim that data never leaves the app.

### Accounts

Users sign in with Apple or Google through Clerk. Do not claim anonymous use except for Demo Mode if that behavior is confirmed.

### Payments

Payments or subscriptions are handled through Apple App Store and Google Play billing, with entitlement management through RevenueCat if enabled. Ignite does not directly collect payment card numbers.

### Analytics and tracking

No third-party analytics, advertising, or behavioral tracking should be claimed unless confirmed against the current build. If partner offer clicks are logged internally, disclose that in plain language.

### Deletion and export

Users should be able to delete their account/data and export their data from the app. Final language should match the shipped UI and any third-party deletion limitations.

### Children

Ignite is not directed to children. Because it handles sensitive financial data, public copy should state that the app is intended for adults.
