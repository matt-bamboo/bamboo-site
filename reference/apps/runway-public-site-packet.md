## Public Positioning

**App name:** Runway

**Tagline:** A calmer way to spend in retirement.

**Short description:**  
Runway helps retirees understand how long their savings may last, what to spend each month, and which account to draw from first.

**Long description:**  
Runway is a private, voice-friendly retirement planning companion for people managing their own money. It turns Social Security, pensions, expenses, and savings accounts into a clear monthly drawdown picture. Clara, the app’s AI guide, answers questions in plain English and helps explain the plan. Runway does not connect to banks, move money, or replace a licensed financial advisor.

## Target Users

Runway is built for:

- Retirees and near-retirees managing their own finances
- Older adults who want plain-English guidance without spreadsheet complexity
- People planning monthly retirement withdrawals
- Users who want a voice-friendly, senior-oriented interface
- Retirees comparing income, expenses, account balances, and longevity estimates

## Core Value Proposition

Retirement changes the central money question from “How much can I save?” to “How should I spend without running out?” Runway helps users organize their numbers, estimate how long savings may last, and understand a monthly drawdown plan without requiring bank login or professional jargon.

## Public Feature Summary

### Runway estimate

Runway estimates the year a user’s savings may last based on entered income, spending, and account balances.

### Monthly drawdown plan

The app provides a plain-English monthly plan for which account to spend from first and why.

### Income and expense view

Users can enter Social Security, pensions, other income, recurring bills, and monthly spending goals.

### Account balance tracking

Users manually enter balances for cash, IRA, Roth, taxable brokerage, and other assets.

### Clara AI guide

Clara is an AI guide that can answer questions about the user’s plan in plain English. Clara is not a licensed financial advisor.

### Voice-friendly interface

Runway is designed with large text, high contrast, narration, and voice input where supported.

### Retirement planning tools

Runway may include estimates for RMDs, Social Security delay comparisons, Medicare-related dates, and retirement calendar reminders.

### Demo mode

Users can explore a sample retirement scenario before entering their own information.

## Support Page Draft

### Support overview

Runway is a private, local-first retirement planning app. Because there are no user accounts, support cannot access, reset, or recover your financial data remotely.

For help, contact: info@bamboo.holdings

When contacting support, include:

- Device model
- iOS version
- Runway app version if visible in the app
- A brief description of the issue
- Whether you are using demo data or your own data

Do not include actual account balances, Social Security amounts, or other sensitive financial details in support emails.

### FAQ

**Does Runway connect to my bank?**  
No. Runway does not connect to banks, brokerages, or financial institutions. Users enter balances manually.

**Where is my data stored?**  
Runway stores financial information on the device. When you ask Clara a question, relevant financial context and your message may be sent to an AI service to generate a response.

**Is Clara financial advice?**  
No. Clara is an AI guide, not a licensed financial advisor. Runway provides estimates and educational guidance only.

**How do I update my numbers?**  
Open the area for your money or profile information and update income, balances, bills, or spending targets.

**How do I reset the app?**  
Use the Start Over or reset option in the app if available. Resetting clears locally stored app data and cannot be undone.

**What happens if I delete the app?**  
Local data may be deleted with the app. If device backup is enabled, the operating system may restore some app data depending on backup settings.

**Does Runway work without internet?**  
Core local planning features may work without internet. Clara’s AI responses require a network connection.

**Can I use Runway on Android or web?**  
Do not claim Android or web availability until confirmed by owner.

### Known limitations to disclose

- No bank linking or automatic balance updates
- No licensed financial advice
- AI answers may be incomplete or wrong
- RMD and Social Security estimates are approximations

## Privacy Page Inputs

Use careful privacy language. Do not claim that data never leaves the device because Clara’s AI responses require sending some context to an AI provider.

### Data users may enter or create

Runway may store:

- First name or profile details
- Age or date-of-birth information used for estimates
- Social Security and pension amounts
- Other income sources
- Account balances
- Monthly spending targets and actual spending
- Fixed bills or recurring expenses
- Medicare-related planning fields
- AI chat messages
- Clara memory notes or preferences

### Local storage

Runway is designed to store user-entered financial information locally on the device. It does not require a user account or bank login.

### Third-party AI processing

When a user asks Clara a question, Runway may send the user’s message and a summary of relevant financial context to a third-party AI provider to generate a response. This processing should be disclosed clearly.

## Financial and Legal Cautions

Runway touches financial planning. Public pages must be careful.

Include a disclaimer like:

> Runway is an educational planning tool, not a licensed financial advisor. Clara is an AI guide, not a fiduciary. Estimates may be incomplete or inaccurate. Always verify important decisions with official sources or a licensed professional.

Avoid implying that Runway:

- Provides licensed financial advice
- Guarantees outcomes
- Calculates official RMDs
- Determines Social Security claiming decisions
