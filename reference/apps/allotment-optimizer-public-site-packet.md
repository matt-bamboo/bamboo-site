## Public Positioning

**App name:** Allotment Optimizer

**Tagline:** Shop smarter. Stay within your limit.

**Short description:**  
Allotment Optimizer helps Arizona cannabis consumers plan dispensary trips by comparing product combinations against allotment limits, budgets, deals, and estimated taxes.

**Long description:**  
Allotment Optimizer is a private, on-device planning tool for adult Arizona cannabis consumers. Users can enter available allotment, add products and deals, choose an optimization goal, and compare valid purchase combinations before visiting a dispensary. The app also supports purchase logging, a 14-day medical allotment tracker, saved presets, spending insights, and an Arizona dispensary directory.

## Target Users

Allotment Optimizer is built for:

- Adult Arizona cannabis consumers, 21 and older
- Medical cardholders tracking a rolling allotment window
- Recreational shoppers trying to compare product options
- Shoppers who want to compare deals, budgets, estimated taxes, and gram limits before a dispensary visit

## Core Value Proposition

Arizona cannabis shopping can involve allotment limits, tax estimates, deal rules, product categories, and budget tradeoffs. Allotment Optimizer helps users compare possible carts before they shop, without spreadsheets or manual math.

## Public Feature Summary

### Smart cart optimizer

Users can enter products, available grams, budget constraints, and a shopping goal. The app ranks valid combinations so users can compare options.

### Multiple optimization goals

The optimizer can support goals such as maximizing grams, finding better value, minimizing cost, and comparing product variety.

### Medical and recreational planning

The app supports Arizona-specific medical and recreational planning modes, including estimated tax differences and medical allotment tracking.

### 14-day medical lock tracker

Medical users can log purchases and review which grams are currently locked and when they become available again.

### Deal-aware product entry

Users can account for deals such as BOGO or percentage discounts when comparing products.

### Product library and presets

Users can save product setups or recurring shopping configurations for later use.

### Purchase history and insights

Users can log purchases and review local spending insights, trends, and product history.

### Dispensary directory and browser workflow

Users can browse a curated Arizona dispensary directory and use saved links to help capture deal information for planning.

### Private, device-centered storage

The app is designed around local device storage. No account is required.

### FAQ

**Does the app connect to live dispensary inventory?**  
No. Users enter products manually or capture details from menu pages. The app is a planning tool, not a live inventory system.

**Why do some grams show as locked?**  
For medical-use planning, recent purchases can count against a rolling allotment window. The tracker estimates when logged grams become available again.

**What does “counts toward lock” mean?**  
Users can mark whether a logged purchase should affect their medical allotment tracker.

**Why does tax not exactly match my receipt?**  
The app uses estimated rates and planning assumptions. Actual receipts may differ due to local taxes, discounts, rounding, store policies, or rule changes.

**Can I use this outside Arizona?**  
No. The current app is Arizona-specific.

**Does the app back up or sync data?**  
No. Current data is stored locally on the device. If the app is deleted or the device is replaced, data may be lost unless a future export or backup workflow is used.

**Does the app sell cannabis?**  
No. It does not sell, deliver, process orders, or facilitate cannabis purchases.

**Does the app provide legal, medical, tax, or financial advice?**  
No. It is an informational planning calculator only.
